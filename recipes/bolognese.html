Bolognese

Description

Our spaghetti bolognese is the ideal weekday meal as it's super easy and quick to make. This bolognese dish is simple, but full of flavour. Bon Appétit!

Ingredients

Serves 6

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • half an orange bell pepper
  • 6 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 x 400g tins plum tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 aubergines chopped into small/medium sized chunks
  • 500g minced meat of your choice
  • 100ml red wine
  • 3 tbsp italian herb seasoning
  • 2 tbsp bouillon powder
  • sea salt
  • 400g spaghetti
  • parmesan
  • fresh basil

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 200C and lightly oil a tray before adding aubergine, making sure to spread the pieces out. Roast for 20 minutes, turning at the halfway mark.
  2. Heat a large sized pan on a high flame, before adding the minced meat. Lower the heat and allow the meat to brown all over before removing from the pan and storing in a bowl.
  3. Return the pan to the heat and add olive oil, followed by the onion, carrot, celery, pepper and bay leaves. Cook until the vegetables soften.
  4. Add garlic and cook until slightly golden.
  5. Add minced meat, tomato purée, tinned tomatoes, bouillon and red wine. Then add herb seasoning and salt to your taste. Using a wooden spoon break up the plum tomatoes and stir well.
  6. Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 45 minutes stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.
  7. Add the roasted aubergines and cook for another 30 minutes.
  8. When the sauce is nearly finished, cook 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  9. Drain the spaghetti and drizzle with a bit of olive oil. Serve with the bolognese sauce, parmesan and fresh basil.
